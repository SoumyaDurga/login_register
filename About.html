Mentor Responsibilities

  • Provide guidance and support to mentees.
  • Share knowledge and expertise in relevant areas.
  • Help mentees set goals and develop plans to achieve them.
  • Offer constructive feedback and encouragement.
  • Assist mentees in building professional networks.

Mentee Responsibilities

  • Be open to learning and receiving feedback.
  • Take initiative in seeking guidance and advice.
  • Set clear goals and communicate them to the mentor.
  • Actively participate in mentoring sessions.
  • Show gratitude and respect towards the mentor.
